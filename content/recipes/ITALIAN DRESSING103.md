# ITALIAN DRESSING



#### yields  1 CUP


## ingredients
* 2 TBL lemon juice 
* 2 clove garlic 
* 3/4 tsp dried basil 
* 3/4 tsp dried thyme 
* 3/4 tsp onion powder 
* Black pepper 



## Instructions
In a small jar, combine the oil, vinegar, lemon juice, garlic, dried basil, thyme, onion powder, and pepper and mix that shit up.
