# TAHINI DRESSING



## ingredients
* 1 TBL citrus juice 
* 1/2 tsp soy sauce or tamari 
* 2 TBL toasted sesame oil 
* 3 TBL olive oil 



## Instructions
Pour all the ingredients into a jar and shake that    up. Taste and add more of

whateverthe  you think it needs. Orange, lime, or lemon works best.
